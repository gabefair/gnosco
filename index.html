Share 2 Archive.Today

Preserve the Web — One Share at a Time

About 🔗

Share 2 Archive.Today is a lightweight Android app that lets you preserve important web pages with just a single tap. Share any URL directly to Archive.Today from any app — articles, tweets, posts, videos — and ensure they're saved for future generations.

No ads. No tracking. Just a clean, open-source tool to protect the public record.

Tech Stack 🔗

  • Kotlin DSL
  • Android SDK 34 (UpsideDownCake)
  • Min SDK 19 (Lollipop)
  • Archive.today backend integration
  • Minimalist design, no bloat

How to Use 🔗

  1. Open any app or webpage (browser, Reddit, Twitter, etc.).
  2. Tap "Share" and choose Share 2 Archive.Today.
  3. The app automatically prepares and opens an archive submission page.
  4. Hit "Submit" on Archive.Today (if needed) to complete the archival.

Privacy Policy 🔗

This app does not use, collect, or store any user data, or Personal Identifying Information (PII), or diagnostic data, or debugging data about you or your phone. Seriously, this app just adds a tool to your share menu to open the archived version of a URL.

Features 🔗

  • Instant Archiving: Share URLs from any app to instantly archive them.
  • Smart URL Cleaning: Automatically removes tracking parameters like utm_source, fbclid, and others.
  • YouTube Shorts Handling: Converts YouTube Shorts links into standard video URLs for reliable archiving.
  • Privacy First: Requires no special permissions. No ads, no analytics, no data collection.
  • Open Source: Developed transparently on GitHub with community contributions welcome.
